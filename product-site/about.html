About Desk

Our Story and Leadership

Desk was created to make premium everyday T-shirts accessible, stylish, and dependable.

About Our Store

Desk store focuses on high-quality T-shirts for everyday use. We prioritize comfort, repeat-fit reliability, and contemporary design. Our collections are created for students, working professionals, and fashion-aware shoppers who want trusted basics with modern styling.

From sourcing to packaging, we optimize each stage of the journey to make sure products arrive fast and stay consistent in quality. We are committed to long-term customer satisfaction and continuous product evolution.

Founder: Mr Harsh Kapoor

Mr Harsh Kapoor founded Desk with a clear mission: build an apparel brand where quality, price fairness, and customer trust are equally important. His leadership combines market insight with operational discipline, helping Desk grow while maintaining strong product standards.

Under his direction, Desk has focused on scalable manufacturing, practical design decisions, and responsive customer support. He continues to guide the company toward innovation in fabric quality and better shopper experiences.
